About

Short Bio

Mathias Kraus (b. 1989) is a Professor for Explainable AI in Business Value Creation at the University of Regensburg. His work focuses on interpretable machine learning for high‑stakes domains, especially healthcare, and the creation of business value through data‑driven methods. Previously, he was a junior professor for Data Analytics at FAU (2021–2024) and a postdoctoral researcher and PhD student at ETH Zurich. He holds an M.Sc. in Computer Science and a B.Sc. in Mathematics from the Karlsruhe Institute of Technology.

He has collaborated with international partners—including a research stay at The University of Texas at Austin—and received awards at venues such as CHI, HICSS, and GRASFI.
